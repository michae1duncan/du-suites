Welcome to Duncan University Suites

Located adjacent to the campus of Southern Utah University, Duncan University Suites are the ideal and preferred living environment for female university students.

Surround yourself with a fun, clean atmosphere where students choose not to smoke or drink, yet love the wonderful things of life. You'll enjoy living close to campus while attending school. You will easily make new friends in a safe, clean and comfortable environment.

  • Fun, wholesome roommates
  • Beautifully decorated homes
  • Easy walking distance to classes and shopping
  • Low utility costs
  • Laundry facilities onsite
  • Wireless, high speed internet
  • Large, flat panel high definition TV's provided
  • Cable TV with full channel line-up
  • DVD players
  • Ample storage
  • Central heating and air conditioning
  • Beautiful landscaped yards
