White House Basement


This cute basement apartment can be your special home away from home as you enjoy the educational benefits of Southern Utah University.

The White House Basement has two large bedrooms, each with two twin beds, a large living room, kitchen, bathroom and laundry/storage room for four female students. For your enjoyment outside, is a large yard with lots of green space to enjoy outdoor games and activities.

Amenities include: Large flat screen television, cable TV with full channel line-up, high speed internet, washer and dryer, lots of storage, free parking, beautiful yard, short walk to Southern Utah University and downtown Cedar City.
